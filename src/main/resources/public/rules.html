How to play


First decide who will go first!! But since your opponent for this game now will be
an AI, than it's gonna be you who will go first!!


Place your ship!!



Now it's time for you to choose your ships and then put them in the map that we've already been prepared for you!!
There are 5 ships with different length that you can place! And you can place each ship in the map horizontally or vertically
You can just click on the ship and then move your cursor to the map to give you the "shadowing" of where you will put the ship
after you place the ship, you will be given a coordinate for each ship so that you can remember where you put your ship before.


Call your shot!



On your turn, pick a target coordinate on the map grid and call out its location by letter and number (coordinate)
You can also easily fire by moving your cursor into the coordinate map and hover your cursor into one of the coordinate square
when you call a shot, the map should tell you than whether you call a hit on the opponent ship or hit a miss


It's a Hit!!


If you call a hit, than the picture of the grid square coordinate will change into a fire
If you call a miss, than the picture of the grid square coordinate will change into a flag
Which means that you have already call a shot at that exact place and it's a miss!
Remember that you cannot call a shot twice in any grid square coordinate.
Your task is to sink all the 5 ships by calling a shot and a hit at all ships that your opponent has!


Winning the game


The first player who can sink all 5 ships of the opponent will win the game!!
